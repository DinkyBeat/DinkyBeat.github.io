Problems I am interested in (2026)

Exploratory questions in algebra, combinatorics, and geometry that remain genuinely unresolved.
Pr1(DeterministicFactorization). Let $\mathbb{F}_q$ be a finite field and $f(x)\in\mathbb{F}_q[x]$ nonconstant. Does there exist a deterministic polynomial-time algorithm for factoring arbitrary polynomials in $\mathbb{F}_q[x]$?
Pr2(StanleyDepth). For a monomial ideal $I\subset k[x_1,\dots,x_n]$, is it always true that \[ \operatorname{sdepth}(I)\ge \operatorname{depth}(I)? \]
Pr3(ReflexiveUnimodality). For a reflexive lattice polytope $P\subset\mathbb{R}^d$ with \[ \sum_{m\ge0} |mP\cap\mathbb{Z}^d|t^m=\frac{h^*(t)}{(1-t)^{d+1}}, \] must the symmetric sequence $(h_i^*)$ always be unimodal?
Pr4(IDPUnimodality). Does the integer decomposition property imply unimodality or stronger positivity properties for the Ehrhart $h^*$-vector?
Pr5(WilfSemigroup). For a numerical semigroup $S=\langle a_1,\dots,a_e\rangle$ with Frobenius number $F$ and genus $g$, does \[ F+1\le e\,g \] always hold?
Pr6(CasasAlvero). If a degree $n$ polynomial $f(x)$ over characteristic $0$ satisfies $\gcd(f,f^{(i)})\neq1$ for all $1\le i\le n-1$, must $f(x)=(x-a)^n$?
Pr7(ZariskiCancellation). If $A[t]\cong k[x_1,\dots,x_{n+1}]$ as $k$-algebras, must $A\cong k[x_1,\dots,x_n]$?
Pr8(RationalVsUnirational). In higher dimensions, when does unirationality imply rationality for projective varieties?
Pr9(NagataInterpolation). For general points in $\mathbb{P}^2$ with multiplicities $m_i$, what is the minimal degree $d$ of a curve vanishing to order $m_i$ at each point?
Pr10(GroupRingTorsion). Is every torsion unit in $\mathbb{Z}[G]$ conjugate in $\mathbb{Q}[G]$ to an element of $G$?
Pr11(GenericInitialIdeals). Can one describe $\mathrm{gin}(I)$ or its Betti numbers explicitly for ideals generated by general forms?
Pr12(CellularResolutions). Which monomial ideals admit minimal free resolutions supported on CW-complexes?
Pr13(gConjectureSpheres). Which sequences occur as the $h$-vector of a simplicial sphere?
Pr14(PermutationBinomials). When does a sparse binomial $x^a+cx^b\in\mathbb{F}_q[x]$ define a permutation of $\mathbb{F}_q$?
Pr15(SparsePermutationPolynomials). Is there a structural classification for permutation trinomials or quadrinomials over finite fields?
Pr16(AlmostCompleteIntersectionHilbert). What Hilbert functions and Betti tables occur for almost complete intersections?
Pr17(SymbolicEquality). When does $I^{(m)}=I^m$ hold for an ideal $I$?
Pr18(SymbolicContainment). For which $m,r$ does \[ I^{(m)}\subseteq I^r \] hold?
Pr19(RegularityAsymptotics). Given $\mathrm{reg}(I^m)=am+b$ for $m\gg0$, how can one determine $a$ and $b$ from the generators of $I$?
Pr20(ReesEquations). For the Rees algebra \[ \mathcal{R}(I)=\bigoplus_{m\ge0} I^m t^m, \] what are its defining equations and homological properties?
